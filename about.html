About

Serious writing, presented clearly.

Perspective is an independent publication built around original research (and shorter peices on whatever fascinates the author) written to be read at whatever depth the reader has time for.

Mission

Make good economics legible without flattening it.

Most economic writing forces a choice: an oversimplified headline or a paper few people will actually open. Perspective tries to remove that trade-off. Every piece of research is published at three depths: beginner, intermediate and advanced plus the original document, so a reader can go as deep as the question deserves.

The site currently does not publish summaries of other people's work or filler content to keep a schedule. Everything here is either an original working paper or an original essay.

Editorial philosophy

Three commitments

01

Show the reasoning

Conclusions are only as useful as the argument behind them. Mechanisms and assumptions are stated, not hidden behind abstract algebra to complicate discussions .

02

Kowow the trade-offs

Economics problems rarely have a free lunch. Costs, uncertainty and downsides are treated as an equally important part of the answer.

03

Write for the reader's time

Academic and opinion peices are often labourious, and not enjoyable to read. But we know the benefits of reading and understanding new knowledge. So a non time consumer manner to read knowledge is alwasys better.
