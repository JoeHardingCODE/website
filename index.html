Working Paper · No. 001

Labour Market Matching Efficiency in the Age of AI

This paper examines how AI may affect labour market matching efficiency by changing how workers search for jobs, how firms screen applicants, and how quickly vacancies and people are matched. It considers the possibility that AI improves allocation, but also the adjustment costs and frictions that may arise during adoption.
